Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vāṇī. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Vāṇī (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from your device.

2.1 Local Data Storage

All data associated with the App is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your name (if you choose to enter it for personalization)
  • Your theme preferences
  • Your favorite verses
  • Your display preferences and settings
  • Your notification preferences

This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

2.2 No Data Collection

We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect device information
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Share data with third parties

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information from your device, we do not use any information for any purpose. All app functionality operates entirely on your device using locally stored data.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms:

  • UserDefaults: Used for storing app preferences and settings
  • App Groups: Used to share data between the main app and widget extension (all data remains on your device)

Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features. We do not have access to any of your data, and it cannot be accessed by us or any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect, track, or analyze user data. We do not integrate with:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any other data collection services

6. Widget Extension

The App includes a widget extension that displays verses on your Home Screen and Lock Screen. The widget:

  • Only accesses data stored locally on your device
  • Does not transmit any data
  • Uses App Groups to share data with the main app (all data remains on your device)
  • Does not collect any information

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Since we do not collect any information, there is no risk to children's privacy.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can delete the App at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • You can change your preferences and settings at any time within the App
  • You can clear your favorites and reset the app to default settings

To delete all data, simply delete the App from your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
