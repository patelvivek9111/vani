Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings → Themes and select your preferred theme. The app will update immediately.

How do I add a widget to my Home Screen?

Long press on your Home Screen, tap the "+" button, search for "Vāṇī", and choose your preferred widget size.

How do I enable personalization?

Go to Settings → Account, enter your name, then enable personalization in Settings → Personalization.

How often do verses change?

Verses change based on your schedule preference (once or twice per day). You can change this in Settings → Verse Schedule.

Can I save favorite verses?

Yes! Tap the heart icon on any verse to save it to your Favorites. Access them from the Favorites tab.
